About Us

Our History

Batterly was founded by two entrepreneurial students who identified a need for convenient, affordable, and enjoyable baked goods among their peers. Recognizing that many students lack access to traditional ovens, they developed a unique solution: creating high-quality desserts using air fryers. What began as a simple idea has evolved into an innovative venture dedicated to making baking more accessible.

Our Mission

Batterly's mission is to deliver affordable, high-quality air fryer baked goods that combine convenience, taste, and value, with a strong focus on meeting the needs of students.

Our Purpose

Our purpose is to make baking easy and accessible for everyone by providing ready-to-use air fryer baking kits, allowing customers to enjoy delicious homemade treats with minimal effort and equipment.
